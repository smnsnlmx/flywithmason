About Me

Flying has always felt like an impossible dream to me. Yet, after earning my PhD in Physics and pursuing a career in Finance, I decided to start flight training—and discovered where my true passion lies. My academic background and professional experience allow me to connect with both younger learners and those with rich life experience. I would be thrilled to help you achieve your goals and become a safe, capable pilot.

Let’s take to the skies!

Structured And Flexible

Everyone is unique, and flight training should reflect that. Based on established training syllabi, a personalized approach can not only expedite the training process and help students save time and money, but also make the journey enjoyable.

Be Safe

Safety is our top priority. I want you to be and feel safe during flight training.

Be Knowledgeable

Ground knowledge is just as important as flying skills, and I will make sure your ground knowledge is solid. Having good ground knowledge will help you pass the checkride, make good decisions, and become a better pilot.

Be Smart

The Redbird Full Motion Simulator (AATD) at KSEE is an excellent tool for certain areas of flight training, especially for Private and Instrument students, allowing them to save time and money. Hours logged in the simulator may be counted toward the aeronautical experience required for ratings.
